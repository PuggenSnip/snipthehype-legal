Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Snip The Hype is a personal, single-user tool that posts its operator's own videos to the operator's own social media accounts. It has no users other than its operator, so it collects no personal data from anyone else.

1. What is stored

The tool stores OAuth access tokens for the operator's own YouTube, Instagram and TikTok accounts, plus basic technical metadata about its own uploads (video ids, view counts, timestamps). Everything is stored locally on the operator's computer.

2. What is NOT collected

No data about other people is collected, stored, sold or shared. There are no analytics, no trackers, no cookies, no advertising and no third-party data processors.

3. TikTok data

Data received from TikTok's APIs (the operator's own account info and post statuses) is used solely to publish and track the operator's own posts, is kept locally, and is deleted when no longer needed. It is never shared with any third party.
